==================================
Hide unused transmit method fields
==================================

* Hides the customer transmit method field on the partner form if the partner is 
  not a customer, and the same for supplier transmit method.
* Intended to reduce errors where users accidentally type in customer transmit
  methods for suppliers and vice versa
